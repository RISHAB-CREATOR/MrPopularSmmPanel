What to choose: universal social networks or the specialized Flickr

On regular social networks such as Facebook, Instagram, Tiktok, and others, not only photos are posted, but also texts, videos, stories, etc. That is, the features of such sites are not tailored specifically for photo content.

The audience there is not the one that wants to constantly check someone's photos, leave thoughtful comments, but somewhat different. On such social networks, people just communicate and share with friends how they spend their time.

Flickr is different. The emphasis here is on photos, so for those who want to show the world their pictures and expect an adequate reaction, this resource is more suitable. Accordingly, Flickr is the choice of pro photographers, amateur photographers, photo salons and photo studios, as well as some companies. And the rest of the social networks is for the general public.
