How a channel or a group can benefit from buying likes on Viber

What is the best indicator of quality content to people? Of course, real likes! The more hearts or thumbs up next to a post or a photo there are, the better other users think of it. As a result, they decide to like, share, subscribe or comment, if possible.

While this is true... The organic increase of likes can be very slow or absent altogether if a community is not promoted in any way. To initiate and accelerate promotion, the owners of channels and groups on Viber buy promotion. After they buy a certain number of likes, the process goes on its own without outside help. People come and like posts because they really care about them.

Why bloggers and entrepreneurs buy likes

Online stores and companies create pages on Viber in order to attract new customers through them, inform and remind former customers about themselves, and improve their reputation. However, on order to attract potential buyers or customers, they need advertising. Unfortunately, it takes a lot of money to do this. So, to increase the level of conversion of ordinary visitors from ads to real customers, they buy promotion.

We are talking about the so-called "social proof". This is when one person follows the example of the majority. Let's say you set up an ad campaign. Someone visits your channel and sees that there are almost no subscribers on it, and the posts either have no likes at all, or one or two. What will the visitor do? In 8 or 9 cases out of 10, they will leave and not do the targeted action that you expected. That is, they will not follow the channel, visit the site, and place an order or purchase.

Now imagine the behavior of such a person, when they see several thousand subscribers on the channel and decent activity in the form of views, likes and shares! Those people who pass by an empty channel will not repeat this mistake if the community is alive. This implies that buying promotion helps businesses and bloggers to attract and retain their target audience that will buy something and let them grow in the future.

If we talk about the blogger's community, then the more real subscribers there are in a group or a channel, the more advertisers will want to order advertising from them.

And this means an increase in earnings and channel popularity. By the way, buying services is needed only for initial promotion. Then, you need to attract real subscribers, otherwise the deception will be revealed and there will be fewer people who would want to buy advertising from you.

How else buying likes on Viber be useful

All members can write messages in Viber groups. If you want to draw attention to your message and let more people know about something, or lure other people's subscribers to your community, likes are what you need. The more likes on a post, the more people will notice and react to it.

Also, likes may be needed for participants in contests, the winners of which are determined by the number of thumbs up. If you want to beat other participants and are ready to win in a not fair way, buy likes and track their addition in real time! Just keep in mind that your competitors can use the same trick. And then it will grow into a competition, the so-called arms race, only inside Viber.

Thanks to promotion services, you can also win a bet. For example, you bet a friend or girlfriend that your photo or text will get 500 likes. Your friend does not believe and claims that it is impossible, but you convince him that it can be done.

Buying likes will help resolve this argument in your favor. This is especially useful for those who bet something substantial.
